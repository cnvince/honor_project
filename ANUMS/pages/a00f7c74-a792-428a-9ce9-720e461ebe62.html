ASSDA is Moving

The Australian Social Science Data Archive is moving to become part of a new service, the Australian Data Archive (ADA), and over the next few weeks you will be automatically redirected to the new ADA service. ADA will contain all of the data and services currently available through the ASSDA website, along with new material and data formats. Remember to bookmark the new site, located at at http://www.ada.edu.au, once you have been redirected.

The ASSDA Data catalogue is now fully available, although we are experiencing some difficulties with broken links to related materials. Please contact the archive at assda@anu.edu.au if you have material you cannot access due to a broken link.
