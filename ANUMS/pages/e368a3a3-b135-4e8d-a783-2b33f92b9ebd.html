Animal Services

We provide animal services and have a proud tradition in supporting cutting edge research. This includes the 1996 Nobel Prize for medicine and physiology winning work undertaken by Professors Peter Doherty and Rolf Zinkernagel in the 1970s. The discoveries concerning the specificity of the cell mediated immune defence that led to their award, were supported by animal services and the staff involved in managing research animal activities.

Currently the facility supports world-leading research which aims to understand genes and mechanisms involved in autoimmunity, cancer, host responses to infectious disease, development and neuroscience. We support projects from all over Australia and internationally including America, Britian, and China.
