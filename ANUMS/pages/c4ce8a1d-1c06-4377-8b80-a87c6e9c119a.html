Dr Robert Tait

PhD, BSc (Hons)
Visiting Fellow
ANU College of Medicine, Biology and Environment
E: robert.tait@curtin.edu.au
Flag this profile

Areas of expertise

  • Mental Health 111714
  • Other Medical And Health Sciences 1199

Research interests

Development of internet interventions for alcohol and other drug problems.
Relationship between substance use and mental health / cognition in the general population (PATH study)

Biography

From 1st May 2013, I will be based at the National Drug Research Institute, Perth WA.

robert.tait@curtin.edu.au

The Effects of Maternally Administered Sustained-Release Naltrexone on the Developing Rat Brain (PhD - awarded 2011)

The Impact of Admission to a ‘No Smoking’ Hospital on Motivation to Quit Smoking: A Comparison of Individuals With and Without Mental Health Disorders (BMed Sc - awarded 2009)
