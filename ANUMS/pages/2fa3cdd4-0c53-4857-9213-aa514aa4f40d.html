List of Fees and Charges for 2013
NOTE: The Student Contribution Amounts, Domestic Tuition Fees, and International Tuition Fees published on this website apply to the 2013 academic year only. For those students continuing or commencing a course of study after 2013, a new schedule of fees will be published by 1 October of the preceding year (eg. by 1 October 2013 for 2014, 1 October 2014 for 2015 etc). All domestic and international tuition fee paying students continuing in their current program of study will not pay more than a 5% increase, compounded each year, from the year of commencement of their program. Select your fee category and the commencement year for your current program to retrieve the applicable fee amount for the 2013 academic year.
