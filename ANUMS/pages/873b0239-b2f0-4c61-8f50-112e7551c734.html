Uploaded on Sep 12, 2010

900 young Canberrans join with hundreds of thousands of others around the nation in a giant synchronised singing event to promote music education. The event, 'Music: Count Us In' is Australia's biggest annual school music event and highlights the value of music education in Australian schools. This video was recorded at The Australian National University on 2 September 2010.

The ACT massed choir was drawn from 11 local schools. 'Music. Count Us In' is now in its fourth year. Each year the event encourages students, teachers and communities all around Australia to simultaneously sing and play the same song.




The ACT leg of this year's event was hosted by multi-award winning musician, Melinda Schneider and is one of four to be 'live streamed' to audiences across Australia. It also features the Band of the Royal Military College, Duntroon, led by Major Geoff Grey.




This video was recorded and produced by Ronin Films: www.roninfilms.com.au
