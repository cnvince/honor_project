CSIRO Student Vacation Scholarships application deadline

Back to Calendar
When:
September 7, 2012 all-day
Contact:
Kelly Byrne, Human Resources Advisor+61 2 9490 8118E-mail
Categories:
CSIRO
Tags:
opportunity

Applications are now open for CSIRO 2012 Enterprise Services Vacation Scholarship Program, this year there are opportunities for 2nd & 3rd year University students with the opportunity to work on real-world problems in a leading R&D organisation. Participation in the Vacation Scholarship Program has influenced previous scholarship holders in their choice of further study and future career options.

The following positions are available (please click on the link to be taken to the advertisement):

Intellectual Property & Licensing – (5 different positions)

Business Development

International

Discovery Centre – Communications

Discovery Centre – Evaluations

Human Resources – Information Sciences Group

Human Resources – Indigenous Engagement & Work Experience Projects

Human Resources – Strategy & Organisational Development

 

Application deadline is 7th September 2012.

Please check individual position for application deadline.

All applications need to meet the essential selection criteria & students must apply online via our CSIRO Careers Webpage.
