Dr Antoine Benard

PhD
Post-doctoral fellow
ANU College of Physical and Mathematical Sciences
E: antoine.benard@anu.edu.au
T: +61 (0) 498102322
Flag this profile

Areas of expertise

  • Inorganic Geochemistry 040202
  • Isotope Geochemistry 040203
  • Igneous And Metamorphic Petrology 040304
  • Volcanology 040314
  • Mineralogy And Crystallography 040306

Research interests

- Mantle wedge petrology and geochemistry
- Arc magmas evolution from the mantle wedge to the lower arc crust
- Volatile abundances, speciation and recycling processes in the mantle wedge
- Relationship between deep magmatic and metasomatic subduction zone processes, oxidation state and economically important elements distribution
- Magma-sediment interaction in soft-substrate environment
- Speciation of transition metals in silicate melt and glasses - relationship with partitioning coefficients

Biography

 

  • October 2011-August 2012 Research and teaching assistant (petrology, mineralogy, mapping, geochemistry, field trips) at the University of Saint-Étienne, France (95 hours of teaching)
  • 2008-2011 Ph.D. with honours at the University of Saint-Étienne and LMV. Research subject: ‘Magmatism, metasomatism and recycling above a subduction zone, insights from mantle xenoliths from the andesitic Avachinsky volcano (Kamchatka, Russia)’. Supervisor: Prof. Dmitri A. Ionov
  • 2008 M.Sc. with honours at the Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris (IPGP), Paris, France. Research internship subject: ‘Compositional impact on structural environment of nickel in silicate glasses, geochemical implications’. Supervisors: Dr. Laurence Galoisy and Prof. Georges Calas
  • 2007 Honours B.Sc. with honours at the Institut Universitaire Européen de la Mer (IUEM), Brest, France. Research internship subject: ‘Magma sediment mingling in a late-ordovician volcano’. Supervisor: Dr. Martial Caroff
