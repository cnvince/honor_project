Student research opportunities

The Australian National University is one of the world's great research universities. We teach what we research and this gives you a unique learning experience, whether this is during an 8-12 week summer project, an honours year to round off your undergraduate degree, or for a higher qualification like the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) or the Master of Philosophy (MPhil).

We have research opportunities in a wide range of fields across engineering and computer science. Once you identify a project or supervisor that matches your interests, you can apply using the links on the right.
