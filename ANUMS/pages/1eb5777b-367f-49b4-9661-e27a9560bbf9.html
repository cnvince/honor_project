Published on Nov 21, 2012

In this second episode of 'Inside the Researchers Lab' Professor Alistair Rendell, Deputy Dean of the ANU College of Engineering and Computer Science, questions guest speakers Associate Prof Heike Ebendorff-Heidepriem from the School of Chemistry and Physics at the University of Adelaine and Prof Chennupati Jagadish from the ANU College of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, about what makes good research.

This series of colloquia aims to provide insights into successful research by successful researchers.

In the style of 'Inside the Actors Studio', and modelled on Proust's Questionnaire, each of our guests goes head-to-head and answers 10 pithy questions on what makes a good research problem.
