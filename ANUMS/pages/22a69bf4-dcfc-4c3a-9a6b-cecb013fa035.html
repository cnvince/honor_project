Biography

1972-79                                     Lecturer/Senior Lecturer/ Principal Lecturer Polytechnic of Central London

1979-1992                     Lecturer/ Senior Lecturer/ Reader University of Essex

1992-96                         Professor, University of Strathclyde

1996-2008                     Professor, University of Birmingham

2008-2010                     Director, Research School in Social Sciences, ANU

 

2010-to date                   Professor, School of Sociology, RSSS, ANU
