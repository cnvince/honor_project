Publications

  • Koch, H & Hercus, L 2013, 'Obscure vs. transparent cognates in linguistic reconstruction', in Robert Mailhammer (ed.), Lexical and Structural Etymology, Walter de Gruyter, Germany, pp. 33-52.
  • Hercus, L 2013, 'Archaisms in placenames in Arabana-Wangkangurru country', in Robert Mailhammer (ed.), Lexical and Structural Etymology, Walter de Gruyter, Germany, pp. 313-322.
  • Hercus, L, Miller, G, Monaghan, P et al 2010, A Dictionary of the Wirangu Language of the Far West Coast of South Australia, Tjutjunaku Worka Tjuta and University of Adelaide, Adelaide.
  • Hercus, L 2009, 'Murkarra, a landscape nearly forgotten: The Arabana country of the noxious insects, north and northwest of Lake Eyre', in Harold Koch and Luise Hercus (ed.), Aboriginal Placenames: Naming and Re-Naming the Australian Landscape, ANU ePress, Canberra Australia, pp. 257-272.
  • Koch, H & Hercus, L 2009, 'Introduction: Old and new aspects of Indigenous place-naming', in Harold Koch and Luise Hercus (ed.), Aboriginal Placenames: Naming and Re-Naming the Australian Landscape, ANU ePress, Canberra Australia, pp. 1-5.
  • Hercus, L, Beckett, J & Martin, S 2008, Mutawintji, Office of the Registrar, Aboriginal Land Rights Act, New South Wales.
  • Hercus, L 2004, 'In the Simpson Desert', in Val Donovan and Colleen Wall (ed.), Making Connections: A journey along Central Australian Aboriginal trading routes, Arts Queensland, Queensland, pp. 47-58.
  • Hercus, L, Simpson, J & Hodges, F, eds, 2002, The Land is a Map. Placenames of Indigenous Origin in Australia, Pandanus Books, Canberra.
  • Hercus, L & Simpson, J 2001, 'The Tragedy of Nauo', in Simpson J, Nash D, Laughren M, Alpher B, Austin P (ed.), Forty Years On: Ken Hale and Australian Languages, Pacific Linguistics, Canberra, Australia, pp. 263-290.
  • Potezny, V & Hercus, L 1999, 'Finch versus Finch-Water: A Study of Aboriginal Place-Names in South Australia', Records of the South Australian Museum, vol. 31 Part 2, pp. 165-180.
