Dr Joanna Sikora

Senior Lecturer in Sociology
ANU College of Arts and Social Sciences
E: joanna.sikora@anu.edu.au

Research interests

Women in Science; Adolescents and their occupational expectations;  Educational inequalities; Social stratification and mobility; Sociology of the family, Social inequality in comparative perspective

Current student projects

Changing Attitudes toward Immigrants: The Influence of the Ageing Population

Malaysian Students in Australia: The Pursuit of Upward Mobility and Permanent Residence
