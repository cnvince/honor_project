Uploaded on Feb 24, 2010

Some of Australias top climate change specialists with expertise covering fields such as international climate policy, meteorology, oceanography, environment, sustainability and risk management deliver an up to date assessment of the current and future climate warnings confronting the planet. Panellists include 2009 Copenhagen Conference attendees Ian Fry, UN spokesperson for the Alliance of Small Island States, and Jenny Goldie from Sustainable Population Australia, who will review the outcomes of the negotiations.

The forum was held as a part of the Australian Meteorological and Oceanographic Society (AMOS) 17th National Conference 27-29 January 2010 at The Australian National University.

Speakers include:

Professor David Karoly (Chair), Professor of Meteorology, University of Melbourne

Professor Matthew England, Professor of Oceanography, University of NSW

Dr Maxine Cooper, ACT Commissioner for Sustainability and Environment

Dr John Schneider, Head, Risk and Impact Analysis Group, Geoscience Australia

Ian Fry, UN spokesperson for the Alliance of Small Island States on matter relating to land use, land use change and forestry, including climate change

Jenny Goldie, National Secretary of Sustainable Population Australia

This forum is part of the TOYOTA-ANU Public Lecture Series.
