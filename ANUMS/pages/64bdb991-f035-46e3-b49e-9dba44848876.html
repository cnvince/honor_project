Title: Data Tables from the Data Management Practices Survey
Author(s): Henty, Margaret
Issue date: 19-Jun-2008
Description: 
The results available in this package of files derive from a survey conducted by three Australian universities on data management practices in late 2007. The surveys were conducted separately within the three universities with a view to collecting information that would assist in the formation of services to support the improvement of data management and the provision of infrastructure within each university. The results shown here are an aggregation, and text comments have been amended where necessary to ensure anonymity for both respondent and their institution.
