Impact case studies from CSIRO


CSIRO’s Performance and Evaluation Team recently completed a series of case studies of delivered impact. They developed ten one pagers on topics such as the Hendra virus, wireless LAN, weather and body armour. Each case study looks at the challenge, the response, the engagement and the impact of the research.

For more information see the  CSIRO Impact case studies website or contact Mark Bazzacco at mark.bazzacco@csiro.au or on +61 2 6276 6534

Case studies are: Aqua Feed Prawn Breeding, Body Armour, Cotton, Health, Hendra Virus, Manufacturing, Murray Darling Basin System, Weather, WLAN, Cotton, Coal Mines
