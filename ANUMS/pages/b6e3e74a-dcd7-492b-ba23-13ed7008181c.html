Graduate_Study@ANU : A Logical Choice!

Dr. Jochen Trumpf and Dr. Alistair Rendell (CECS, NICTA)

SUMMER SCHOOL SEMINAR

DATE: 2007-12-12
TIME: 16:30:00 - 17:30:00
LOCATION: Physics and Phsychology G6 lecture theatre, building 38, ANU campus
CONTACT: JavaScript must be enabled to display this email address.

ABSTRACT:
The ANU offers a host of opportunities for graduate study. And as home to the Institute of Advanced Studies (which includes RSISE), plus strong ties to NICTA and the CSIRO ICT centre ANU is an excellent place to study mathematics/computer science/engineering.

In this talk we discuss graduate study. What it means to undertake a researh degree, what skills you will gain, how you determine a project, how you go about applying, how poor you will be (!) etc. Our aim will be to have an informal presentation, with plenty of opportunity for questions from the floor.
BIO:
Dr. Jochen Trumpf, Associate Dean (Research Training) CECS Associate Director for Education, NICTA (Canberra) and Dr. Alistair Rendell, Associate Dean (Research) CECS
