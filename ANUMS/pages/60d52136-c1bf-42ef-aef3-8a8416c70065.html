Published on Sep 12, 2012

2012 marks 50 years of European Union and Australian relations. In a series of discussions Ambassadors and/or High Commissioners from EU Member States, together with Australian Ambassadors to Europe and other experts, gather at the ANU Centre for European Studies to discuss why they joined the EU, their current position and how their country interacts with Australia.

In this episode H.E. Mr Enrique Viguera, Ambassador for Spain to Australia, H.E. Mr Krassimir Stefanov, Ambassador for Bulgaria to Australia and New Zealand, and Mr Noel Campbell, Official Secretary and Chief of Staff, Office of the Governor of New South Wales, discuss the fifty years of relations between Australia and the EU. The discussion is moderated by Mr Paul Bongiorno, National Affairs Editor at Network Ten.
