ANU Experts Guide


Find an expert

please note that multiple words cannot be used in a single search

The ANU Experts Guide is a searchable database of academic staff at The Australian National University available for interview on areas within their research and teaching expertise.

The guide can be searched in the following ways:

  1. Using the drop down list of subjects.
  2. Entering a keyword (please note that multiple words cannot be used in a single search).
  3. Combining these methods to perform a search using both a specific subject and a specific keyword.

Experts listed have chosen to make themselves available to media in their area of expertise, and many of our experts have their home and/or mobile contact numbers listed. However, if you need further help finding an ANU expert, please contact the Strategic Communications and Public Affairs office on our media hotline on 02 6125 7979 (staffed inside and outside business hours) or email media@anu.edu.au.

Please bear in mind that while the academics on this list are happy to speak to journalists, they do have heavy teaching and research commitments and may not always be available for interview.
