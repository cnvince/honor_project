Biography

Terence (Terry) Hull is Professor of Demography in the Australian Demographic and Social Research Institute (ADSRI) and Adjunct Professor of the National Centre for Epidemiology and Population Health (NCEPH). In the latter position he holds the JC Caldwell Chair in Population, Health and Development. More information.
