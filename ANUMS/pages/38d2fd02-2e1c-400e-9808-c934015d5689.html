Dr Avery Andrews

Senior Lecturer (Linguistics)
ANU College of Arts and Social Sciences
E: Avery.Andrews@anu.edu.au
Flag this profile
Jump to: Biography|Publications|Related websites

Areas of expertise

  • Linguistic Structures (Incl. Grammar, Phonology, Lexicon, Semantics) 200408

Research interests

 

Grammatical Typology

Syntax-Semantics Interface (Glue semantics)

Syntax-Morphology Interface (Lexical-Functional Grammar)

 

Biography

Dr Avery Andrews is a Senior Lecturer in the Linguistics Program, School of Language Studies, Faculty of Arts. He is well known for his work on syntactic and morphological theory.

Publications

  • Andrews, A 2012, 'UG and variation in expression', in Cathryn Donohue, Shunichi Ishihara, William Steed (ed.), Quantitative approaches to problems in linguistics: Studies in honour of Phil Rose, Lincom Europa, Munich, Germany, pp. 223-240.
  • Andrews, A 2010, 'Propositional glue and the projection architecture of LFG', Linguistics and Philosophy: a journal of natural language syntax, semantics, logic, pragmatics, and processing, vol. 33, no. 3, pp. 141-170.
  • Andrews, A 2010, ''Grammatical' vs. 'Lexical' Meaning Constructors for Glue Semantics', Conference of the Australian Linguistic Society 2009, ed. Yvonne Treis and Rik De Busser, Australian Linguistics Society, Melbourne Australia, pp. 34 pp.
  • Mistica, M, Andrews, A, Arka, I et al 2009, 'Double Double, Morphology and Trouble: Looking into Reduplication in Indonesian', Australasian Language Technology Association Workshop (ALTA 2009), ed. Luiz Pizzato and Rolf Schwitter, Australasian Language Technology Association, Sydney, pp. 44-52.
  • Andrews, A 2008, 'Splitting vs. lumping in morphological analysis: evidence from Greek', in Claire Bowern, Bethwyn Evans & Luisa Miceli (ed.), Morphology and Language History: In honour of Harold Koch, John Benjamins Publishing Company, Amsterdam, The Netherlands, pp. 91-97.
  • Andrews, A 2008, 'The Role of PRED in LFG+Glue', International Lexical-Functional Grammar Annual Conference (LFG 2008), ed. Kirsti Borjars and Martin Forst, CSLI Publications, Stanford, USA, pp. 46-67.
  • Andrews, A 2007, 'Input and Glue in OT-LFG', in Annie Zaenen, Jane Simpson, Tracy Holloway King, Jane Grimshaw, Joan Maling (ed.), Architectures, Rules, and Preferences; variations on themes by Joan W. Bresnan, CSLI Publications, USA, pp. 319-340.
  • Andrews, A 2007, 'The major functions of the noun phrase', in Timothy Shopen (ed.), Language Typology and Syntactic Description: Clause Structure (2nd ed), Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, United Kingdom, pp. 132-223.
  • Andrews, A 2007, 'Relative Clauses', in Timothy Shopen (ed.), Language Typology and Syntactic Description - Complex Constructions (2nd ed), Cambridge University Press, New York, USA, pp. 206-236.
  • Andrews, A 2006, 'Semantic Composition for NSM, Using LFG+Glue', Conference of the Australian Linguistic Society 2005, ed. Keith Allan, Australian Linguistics Society, online, pp. 1-22.
  • Andrews, A 2005, 'Homeric Recitation, with Input from Phonology and Philology', Antichthon, vol. 39, pp. 1-28.
  • Andrews, A 2004, 'Glue logic vs. Spreading Architecture in LFG', Conference of the Australian Linguistics Society 2003, ed. Christo Moskovsky, Australian Linguistics Society, University of Newcastle, pp. 16pp.
  • Andrews, A 2001, 'Non-canonical A/S Marking in icelandic', in Noonan, Michael (ed.), Non-Canonical Marking of Subjects and Objects, John Benjamins Publishing Company, Amsterdam, pp. 85-111.
