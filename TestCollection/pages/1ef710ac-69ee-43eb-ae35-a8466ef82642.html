Published on Dec 13, 2012

Prof Bob Bowker sits down with Prof Amin Saikal to discuss the publication of his new book, entitled 'American Democracy Promotion in the Middle East: From Bush to Obama'. His co-editors are Professor Shahram Akbarzadeh, Dr Benjamin MacQueen and Professor James Piscatori.

Amin Saikal is Professor of Political Science and Director of the Centre for Arab and Islamic Studies (The Middle East and Central Asia) at the Australian National University.
