About us

ANU Marketing Office is custodian of the ANU brand and sets the strategic framework for delivering marketing, communication and brand activity across the University.

We produce University-wide marketing publications and advertising, and assist with design and production of College and Divisional marketing products.

We also approve use of the ANU brand on all marketing, advertising, outreach, communication, merchandise and website products.

» Contact us to find out more

 

About the ANU brand

The Australian National University adopted a new brand in December 2011. The new brand includes a fresh logo, an ANU window design to frame images of ANU activities, a new style of imagery, a more flexible colour scheme and a new font.

» About the brand refresh
» Brand showcase

Templates are available for Word, PowerPoint, HTML emails, websites, and more. Pre-printed stationery (business cards, certificates, letterhead etc.) can be ordered from University Printing.

Merchandise can be ordered with a charge code for ANU use (for conferences, gifts, etc.), and other merchandise (clothing and gifts) can be ordered by staff, students, alumni and the public for personal use.

Public lecture flyers should be discussed with Strategic Communications & Public Affairs.

Logos can be requested if an ANU-branded item is not available.

All other marketing products (brochures, banners, flyers and more) can be requested through the ANU Marketing Office by emailing brand@anu.edu.au.
