Sustainable construction in the EU

28 Nov 2011

The ANU Center for European Studies has published a working paper on sustainable construction regulation in the European Union by Dr. Jeroen van der Heijden (CEGNet) and Dr. Ellen van Bueren (Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands). The working paper finds that changes towards more sustainable construction regulation in the European Union are likely too slow to timely address the ecological risks created by the buildings sector.

The working paper can be found here.
