Dr Luigi Tomba

BA (Venice), PhD (San Marino)
Senior Fellow. Australian Centre on China in the World
ANU College of Asia and the Pacific and Other ANU Non-College Academic
E: luigi.tomba@anu.edu.au
Flag this profile
Jump to: Biography|Publications

Research interests

Chinese contemporary politics and society, labour markets and labour reform, urban politics, urbanization, neighbourhood politics and governance, housing reform, residential segregation, consumption and social classes.

Biography

My main research interest is on Chinese urbanization and urban politics. My work has moved from labour politics to community governance and neighbourhood politics. I have published extensively on the consequences of housing reform and the emergence of a new middle class, and I am now focusing on the practices of urbanization in peri-urban Chinese villages, in particular land management and ther consequences for local governance. In the Australian Centre on China in the World I am leading a stream of research called "China Urban" (http://ciw.anu.edu.au)

Since 2005 I have been the Co-editor, with Andrew Kipnis, of The China Journal, a leading journal on contemporary China, now published with the University of Chicago Press.
