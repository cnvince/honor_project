Uploaded on Feb 23, 2011

The ANU Centre for European Studies hosts a public seminar to mark the 30th Anniversary of relations between the European Union Parliament and the Australian Parliament.

Speakers include visiting Members of the European Parliament, H. E. Mr. David Daly, Ambassador and Head of Delegation of the European Union to Australia and to New Zealand, Mr Harry Jenkins, Speaker of the House of Representatives, Australian Parliament, and Professor Paul Pickering and Dr John Besemeres from the Australian National University's Centre for European Studies.
