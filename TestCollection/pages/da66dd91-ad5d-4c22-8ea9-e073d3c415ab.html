H.C. Coombs Creative Arts Fellowship

  • Share this »

This news story has been kept for historical purposes, and content may now be out of date.

ANU Schools and Centres from relevant areas are invited to nominate a performer for the 2010 H.C. Coombs Creative Arts Fellowship.

The H.C. Coombs Creative Arts Fellowship embraces opportunities for performing, literary and visual artists. Its purpose is to allow the recipient to engage in a period of uninterrupted creative work in residence at ANU, contribute to the fostering of the creative arts within the University, and interact with the academic community, staff, students and visitors.

In 2010 a Creative Arts Fellowship will be offered in the area of performance (including theatre, music, and dance)

For more information about the fellowship and application guidelines please visit http://rsh.anu.edu.au/fellowships/coombs/index.php
Applications close 31 October 2009.
