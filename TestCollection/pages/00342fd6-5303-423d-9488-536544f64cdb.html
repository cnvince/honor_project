Published on Mar 27, 2013

Professor Brian Schmidt and Professor Dan Shechtman discuss what motivates and inspires them, and what the path is like to winning a Nobel Prize.

Professor Dan Shechtman, Technion (Israel Institute of Technology) Professor of Materials Science was awarded the Nobel Prize for Chemistry in 2011 and Professor Brian Schmidt, ANU Professor of Astrophysics was jointly awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 2011.

The discussion is opened by ANU Vice-Chancellor Professor Ian Young and is moderated by Canberra Times Education Reporter Emma MacDonald.
