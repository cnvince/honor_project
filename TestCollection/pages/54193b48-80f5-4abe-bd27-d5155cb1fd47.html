Dr Paul Francis sheds some light on some of life's big questions in this new web series - The Greatest Unsolved Mysteries of the Universe. In the first episode, he addresses the question 'where the Earth came from?'

Each episode will explore a mystery in-depth, in an accessible and light-hearted way. Dr Francis will also be delving into some of the comments left on his previous video to answer some viewers' mysteries. If you have a universal mystery you'd like Paul to have a look at, leave it as a comment on this video. He'll be picking a few to look at throughout the series.

The series builds on his video from 2010, The Greatest Unsolved Mysteries of the Universe.

Dr Paul Francis is an award-winning astrophysics researcher and lecturer at The Australian National University. His research focuses on comets, galaxy formation and novel teaching methods.
