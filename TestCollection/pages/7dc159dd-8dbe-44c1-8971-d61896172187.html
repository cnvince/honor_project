Duties:

Head of School

The role of the Head is to advance the School with the specific aim of achieving world-class excellence in research and teaching in line with ANU goals and the College Strategic Plan. Specifically, this includes:

  • academic leadership of the School, including responsibility for the management, development and quality of the teaching, research and student support services;
  • development and management of the academic and general staff in the School;
  • Development and management of the School's finances and infrastructure; managing and implementing change.
