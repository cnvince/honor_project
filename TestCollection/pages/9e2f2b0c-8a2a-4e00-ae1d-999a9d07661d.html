Published on May 22, 2013

In this audio file, ANU Vice-Chancellor Professor Ian Young speaks with Mark Parton of 2CC Canberra about the $51 million budget cut that ANU will face over the coming years. Professor Young explains how staff and students will be involved in addressing the budget challenge. This audio file is being used with permission from 2CC Canberra.
