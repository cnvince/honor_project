The Caldwell Library is a small library specialising mainly in demographic publications. It is located in room 3031 of the Coombs Building. Access to the library is available to ADSRI staff, students and visitors. Others wishing to use the library should contact the librarian, Jenny White.

Professor John C. Caldwell


The library is named in honour of Emeritus Professor John C. Caldwell who is an internationally renowned demographer and who was Head of Demography at ANU for many years. He is currently an Adjunct Professor with ADSRI.
