Dr Charmian Bennett

Ph.D. (Monash), M.A. (UBC), M.Sc. (Canterbury), B.Sc.(Hons) (Canterbury)
Research Fellow
ANU College of Medicine, Biology and Environment
E: charmian.bennett@anu.edu.au
T: 02 6125 6871
Flag this profile
Jump to: Researcher's projects|Publications|Related websites

Areas of expertise

  • Environmental And Occupational Health And Safety 111705
  • Epidemiology 111706
  • Respiratory Diseases 110203
  • Climatology (Excl. Climate Change Processes) 040105

Research interests

  • Health impacts of climate change (especially heat, aeroallergens and allergic respiratory disease)
  • The environment as a determinant of health (eg: impact of seasonal weather and climate changes on health)
  • Health impacts associated with extreme biochemical events (dust storms, bushfires)
  • Urban air quality and health (especially particulate matter)

Researcher's projects

  • The impact of seasonality on health outcomes in the 45&Up cohort
  • Determining the best heat metric for use in Australian cities
  • The health impacts of heat exposure associated with urban heat islands and climate change
  • Climate change, aeroallergens and respiratory health in Australian cities
