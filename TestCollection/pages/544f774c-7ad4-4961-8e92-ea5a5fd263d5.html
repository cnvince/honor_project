Published on Nov 7, 2012

In this first episode of 'Inside the Researchers Lab' Professor John Hosking, Dean of the ANU College of Engineering and Computer Science, questions guest speakers Hugh Durrant-Whyte, CEO of NICTA, and Professor Bob Williamson from the ANU College of Engineering and Computer Science, about what makes good research.

This series of colloquia aims to provide insights into successful research by successful researchers.

In the style of 'Inside the Actors Studio', and modelled on Proust's Questionnaire, each of our guests goes head-to-head and answers 10 pithy questions on what makes a good research problem.
