Applicants for undergraduate programs
Australian Citizens, Australian Permanent Residents, Australian Humanitarian Visa Holders, New Zealand Citizens and international students who are currently completing an Australian Year 12, an International Baccalaureate in Australia or a New Zealand NCEA Level 3 must apply through Universities Admissions Centre (UAC). Please refer to UAC for information on application deadlines. International applicants who are currently completing an International Baccalaureate outside Australia may apply directly to ANU or through Universities Admissions Centre (UAC).

Application Closing Dates – International Undergraduate and Graduate Coursework
Semester 1 (February) 12 December
Semester 2 (July) 31 May

For details of domestic closing dates, please refer to specific program deadlines on the how to apply page.
Graduate research students may apply at any time. Please note, however, that there are application closing dates for graduate research scholarships.
If you have not lodged or are unable to lodge your application by the relevant closing date, you are encouraged to apply for the following semester intake.

Returning Applicants
If you already have commenced an application with ANU you can return to Login to My ANU to complete and submit your form.

If you have any questions about applying to ANU please contact admissions@anu.edu.au
