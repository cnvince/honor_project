Publications

Department of Political & Social Change (PSC) is home to The Contemporary China Centre which was set up in 1970. The Centre has a thriving variety of publications, but above all it is home to one of the leading international journals on contemporary China, The China Journal.The Centre produced a commemorative series of lecture papers, the George Ernest Morrison Lectures in Ethnology.
