ARC research funding

The Australian Research Council (ARC) is a statutory authority within the Australian Government's Innovation, Industry, Science and Research portfolio. The ARC advises the Government on research matters, manages the National Competitive Grants Program, a significant component of Australia's investment in research and development. The ARC is by far the most important funding agency for the ANU College of Arts and Social Sciences.
