"The Enlightenment to China of E-government Application in Rural Areas from Japan, South Korea and India"

Jinglei Wang, Wenyun Liu

Abstract



E-government is one of the major forms of government informatization. It has played an important role in the rural areas of some Asian countries. This article gives an analysis of e-government application in some rural areas in Asia and the status of e-government application in rural China, and gets some enlightenment to solve our problems.
