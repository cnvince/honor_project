• Andrew Stoler, Associate Commissioner, Productivity Commission
• Paul Gretton, Assistant Commissioner, Productivity Commission

Speaker/Host: Crawford School of Economics and Government, ANU
Venue: Molonglo Theatre, Crawford School (Bld #132)

Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
