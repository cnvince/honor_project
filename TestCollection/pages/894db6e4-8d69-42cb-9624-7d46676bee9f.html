Published on Aug 20, 2012

Dr Ken Henry AC launches 'The China Story Yearbook 2012: Red Rising, Red Eclipse' on Friday 10 August 2012 at The Australian National University.

The China Story Project is a web-based account of contemporary China created by the Australian Centre on China in the World (CIW) at The Australian National University in Canberra, which has the most significant concentration of dedicated Chinese Studies expertise and is the publisher of the leading Chinese Studies journals in Australia.

Other speakers:
- Professor Geremie R. Barmé, Director of the Australian Centre on China in the World (CIW)
- Dr Benjamin Penny, Deputy Director of the Australian Centre on China in the World (CIW)
- Professor Richard Rigby, Executive Director of the ANU China Institute
