The second forum in The Australian National Universitys National Health Reform Series asks whether GP Super Clinics are right way forward for health care in Australia.

The event was facilitated by Mr Robert Wells, the Director of the Australian Primary Health Care Research Institute at ANU. It was held at ANU on Wednesday 24 February 2010.

The forum featured a panel of leading experts including Professor Mark Harris, primary health care researcher from the University of New South Wales; health consumer advocate and Deputy Chairman of the ACT Health Council, Mr Russell McGowan; Dr Rashmi Sharma, President of the ACT Division of General Practice; and Australian Medical Association Federal Vice President, Dr Steve Hambleton.

For more information on the National Health Reform Series go to: http://www.anu.edu.au/aphcri/national...
