  ASIAN STUDIES WWW VIRTUAL LIBRARY   
Established: 24 March 1994. Last updated: 25 Jan 2010

Edited by Dr T. Matthew Ciolek
and a team of co-editors of the AS WWW VL Project.

Over 16 years of continuous service to the online community. The Asian Studies WWW Virtual Library is designed for transmission speed, not fancy looks. Should you have problems with viewing this page with the Internet Explorer, try a Safari or Firefox browser. Since 14 Jun 2002 Asian Studies WWW VL is a sister site of the History WWW VL (Originally at the U. Kansas, US, now at the European University Institute, Florence, Italy).

Google rank #1 among the 10,100,000 asian studies pages world-wide (Jan 2011).
